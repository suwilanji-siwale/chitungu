About us

We are a Zambian mixed crop and animal farm based in Mpika, Muchinga Province. We started farming in 2019 and legally registered with the authorities in 2021. We are specialized in growing chilli. We grow long cayenne, African Bird Eye, scotch bonnet and Habanero. We sell fresh and dry stalkless and stalk chillies. Our Chillies is organically grown and sundried. We have capacity to supply 5 to 10 metric tonnes of both fresh and dry chilli. We also specialize in raring village chickens, growing spices such as marigold, black pepper, rosemary, mint, coriander and other vegetables.
